This organization exists as a place to organize various projects done by teams of University of Prince Edward Island computer science students through the "capstone" CS4810-CS4820 2-semester course.
The projects in this organization are all done with me, the UPEI librarian Melissa Belvadi, acting in the role of "client", providing the project requirements and primary design support and guidance.
The projects included here span multiple years ("cohorts") of student teams taking that course.

In some years, multiple students teams work independently on the same project. And sometimes they each added extra features that I think are pretty useful, leading me to not be able to choose which is the "best". So I include them all here.

In all cases, I am solely responsible for maintaining the code after the students have completed the CS course.
